About SimplySucc

SimplySucc was founded in 2018 as a resource to make indoor succulent care easy and fun! We offer succulent care instructions and information on different types of succulents.

All this website's information comes from the SimplySucc team. The SimplySucc team has years of experience showing TLC to succulents. To learn more about our team, look below!
